About Us

Fusion, a contemporary hair salon is located in the heart of downtown Glen Ellyn.  We are a Full Service Hair Salon, we offer every service from women’s haircuts, men’s haircuts, kid’s haircuts, highlights, lowlights, all over color, and the newest trends in color Ombre&Balayage, and we also offer special occasion styles and updos and Keratin treatments.
Our goal at Fusion Salon is to exceed our client’s expectations and bring our level of talent to your hair care needs. We strive to build relationships with our clients so they receive a hair style customized to their physical features and desires. We take great pride in our Salon and aim to provide a clean, relaxing and comfortable environment.

Fusion salon is unique from other hair salons, our stylists all have their own business’s within Fusion.  To find the right stylist for you, please go to biographies. You will find each stylist services, specialties, price lists and contact information. If you need help finding a stylist please call the salon directly and we will do our best to fit you best with a stylist.

We truly can’t wait to meet you!
